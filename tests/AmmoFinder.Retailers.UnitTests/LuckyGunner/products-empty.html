357 Mag Ammo Quick Facts
  • 357 magnum ammo is one of the few calibers that are used in revolvers, semi-auto, and rifles.. 
  • The 357 mag ammunition was designed in response to early body armor and to defeat car doors.  
  • To find bulk .357 magnum ammunition, use the "Sort By" toggle at the top of the page.
  • 357 magnum ammo is available in grains ranging from 125 to 200 grains.
